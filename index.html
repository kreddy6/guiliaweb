About Me

Born and raised in Brazil, I discovered the magic of ballet at the age of six. What began as childhood curiosity soon became a lifelong passion — one that has carried me from the studios of São Paulo to stages around the world.

My dance draws from the richness of Brazilian culture — its warmth, rhythm, and vibrant energy — blended with the discipline and elegance of classical ballet. Every performance is an invitation to feel something deeply, to connect across language and distance through the universal language of movement.

Whether performing on stage, teaching workshops, or collaborating with fellow artists, I believe dance has the power to transform — both the dancer and the audience.
